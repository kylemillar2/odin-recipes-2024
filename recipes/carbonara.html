Carbonara

Description

  • 1 whole egg and 1 egg yolk
  • Parmesan
  • Guanciale
  • Pepper
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Mix the egg and extra egg yolk in a bowl
  2. Add the parmesan and a pinch of pepper, and mix until combined
  3. Put the pasta on to cook
  4. Fry the guanciale over medium-low
  5. When the pasta is al dente, save some of the water and put the pasta in the pan with the guanciale and turn off the heat
  6. Add the egg mixture and some of the pasta water, mix until smooth
  7. Serve immediately with some more parmesan on top
